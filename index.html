Student Expectations

No programming experience is necessary. We hope to provide a curriculum that is open to all introductory students.

Motivation

Currently, there are not many opportunities to pursue computer science in San Leandro as a secondary school student. At San Leandro High School, one of the instructors took their first CS course in 2013. It was terrible! Instead of learning, the instructor played Tetris and various card games, which led to the instructor to have a negative impression of CS. However, that changed when the instructor went to college. After college, the instructor found computer science to be very interesting and fun. So, the motivation of the course is to provide a good opportunity for young students to learn CS from people who care about technology. Additionally, some of the instructors feel that if students happen to like technology, a career in tech provides one path to socioeconomic mobility that can help vastly improve future outcomes.

Course Description

This is a summer course designed to give high school students an opportunity to learn about computer science so students can learn more computer science on their own. The course will use Python as the programming language of choice and follow three modules involving graphics programming with Processing, data processing with scientific libraries (matplotlib, pandas, numpy), and end with modeling the real world. In the end, students will have a taste of what programming is like, so that they can make their own informed choices about pursuing computer science in the future.

Worksheets

Worksheets will be provided online each week before class, so students have a reference about topics that will be covered. The goal of the worksheets is to provide exercises that will enhance practical skills.

Technology

Preferably, students will bring their own laptops, so that they can can spend extra time outside of the weekly sessions to continue their learning. However, most students may not have access to laptops, so we will do our best to provide the necessary equipment each week.
